  • Strokes

    Strokes aims at building a design community that can bring about a positive change in the status quo through design. Three functional teams work under the banner of STROKES.
    1. Graphic Design team The team here hones and uplifts the visual designing and aesthetic skills of the enthusiasts helping them work with various designing softwares and tools.
    2. UI/UX team: aims at training and uplifting students' overall design thinking skills,human-centric problem solving skills, and also help them learn the demanding skills of designing user friendly interfaces and top notch user experiences.
    3. 3D modelling team Aims at helping students get well versed with various 3D modelling softwares and build an extraordinary skill set of creative jaw dropping 3D assets.
